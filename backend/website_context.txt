Leave Service for Employees
What is the "Leave" user service?
The "Leave" user service refers to the Leave tab accessed through the left bar in Zoho People. It displays all the necessary employee leave related information such as leave summary, leave balance, etc. It allows users to raise leave-related requests, view upcoming holidays, and more. This space is intended for all users and is accessible by all roles (team members, managers and even administrators).
How to navigate to leave user service?
Simply click on the Leave icon in the left bar in Zoho People.
What can you do here?
View leave summary
Navigate to
Leave
>

Calendar View
Use the
Calendar

Sync Leave Calendar
By default, Zoho People is integrated with Zoho Calendar. Enable or disable the setting in
Zoho Calendar
to view your leave and holiday information in Zoho Calendar.
Additionally, Zoho People provides an option to sync your approved leaves and holiday information to
Microsoft 365

From the apply leave screen, you can select the leave type, date range, view the leave summary, access leave reports, read the leave policy document if available, and add attachments if required.
What is a Leave Grant?
In Zoho People, "Leave Grant" allows managers to allocate additional leave days to employees beyond their regular entitlement.
What is Compensatory Off?
In Zoho People, "Compensatory Off" allows employees to take time off in lieu of extra hours or days worked beyond their regular schedule.
Cancelling a leave request
Cancel a leave request by navigating to
Leave
>
Leave Requests

What is Partial Leave Cancellation?
In Zoho People, "Partial Leave Cancellation" allows employees to cancel a portion of their unapproved leave while keeping the rest intact.
View Bradford score information
View Bradford score information from the
Bradford Score
tab (if feature is enabled).
What is Bradford score?
In Zoho People, the Bradford Score measures employee absenteeism by calculating the impact of frequent short-term absences on workforce productivity.

Attendance Service for Employees
What is the "Attendance" user service?
The "Attendance" user service refers to the Attendance tab accessed through the left bar in Zoho People. It displays all the necessary attendance service-related information ,such as attendance summary, overtime, shift, etc. Here you can raise attendance-related requests, check-in and more.
How to navigate to attendance user service?
Simply click on the Attendance icon in the left bar in Zoho People.
What can you do here?
Check-in / Check-out / Break Time
Navigate to
Attendance
>
My Data
>
Attendance Summary
tab and use the
Check-in
/
Check-out
green/red button. Depending on your organization's preferences, the counter for your break time may begin when you mark a check-out during office hours.
View attendance summary
Navigate to
Attendance
>
My Data
>
Attendance Summary
tab.
The Attendance Summary displays the days or hours view of your attendance, this includes hours worked, check-in/check-out time lines. To view a record in detail, simply click on the particular timeline. Use the (
) icon to cycle between the time periods.
View check-in and check-out details
To view check-in and check-out details for a specific workday:
In the Attendance Summary tab, click on List View.
Click on the day for which you want to view the check-in and check-out details.
If facial recognition is enabled, you can view captured images of yourself or your repartees (based on permission) by clicking the profile icon. Images are available for check-ins and check-outs completed via the Zoho People mobile app, web application, or kiosk. For kiosk-based check-ins and check-outs, captured images from the past three months will only be available for viewing in the system.
Raising a permission or on-duty request
Navigate to
Attendance
>
My Data
, click on the Request button and select Permission or On-duty (availability depends on your organization's policies)
You can also track and raise
Permissions
and
On Duty
requests from their corresponding tabs.
Within the Attendance Summary page, you also have
Filter
,
Export
and
Audit History
options.
What is Audit History?
Audit history shows the attendance log timeline along with any changes initiated by any Zoho People user or the system. For example, if your manager updates your attendance entries, this can be viewed here.
Regularize your attendance
What is attendance regularization?
Attendance regularization offers the opportunity to submit requests to correct any inaccurate attendance records. These inaccuracies may arise from instances such as forgetting to check in or out or technical errors resulting in incorrect entries. Through the attendance regularization process, you can submit a request to amend your attendance records by providing the correct information along with an explanation for the request.
Navigate to
Attendance
>
My Data
>
Regularization
tab, view your pending requests, or click
Add Request
to raise a new regularization request. You will be required to select the date, time range, reason, and description when submitting a regularization request. Once your manager approves the regularization request, the system will automatically update your attendance records.
View shift information
Navigate to
Attendance
>
My Data
>
Shifts
tab. Here you can v
iew shift information and assign shifts (if applicable).
You can view shifts in weekly or monthly views. Use the arrows (
) to cycle between the periods.
Hover over the specific day’s shift and click
Edit
icon to request a shift change for that day or a specific period.
You can also view leave information in your shift calendar.
View team members attendance status
Navigate to
Attendance
>
Team
>
Team Members
. Using this, you
can quickly check your team/project members check-in status.
View attendance-related reports
You have access to four Attendance related reports (based on your organization's employee permission preferences), namely, Early/late check-in and check-out,
Present/absent status.
Presence hours break-up,
Overtime details. To access these reports, navigate to Reports (bottom left-corner) and select the corresponding Attendance report.
